Tips for Managing Stress

Practice relaxation techniques, such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga.

Engage in regular physical activity to reduce stress and improve mood.

Take breaks throughout the day to recharge and reset.

Get enough sleep to ensure your body and mind have time to rest and recover.

Talk to someone you trust about your feelings and experiences.

Set realistic goals and prioritize tasks to avoid feeling overwhelmed.

Avoid excessive consumption of caffeine, alcohol, or other substances.

Engage in hobbies or activities that bring you joy and relaxation.

Resources and Support
